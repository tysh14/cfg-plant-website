Our Featured Plant!

Hoya Australis Blondie

The Hoya Australis Blondie is a popular trailing or climbing houseplant with thick, waxy round leaves edged with cream. We love how its cream edges turn into a stunning pink, making it an eye-catching addition to any indoor space. This versatile plant thrives in various light conditions, from bright indirect light to lower light environments, and is relatively low-maintenance, making it an excellent choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts. Its ability to trail gracefully from hanging baskets or climb support structures also makes it a perfect option for creating vertical interest in your home decor.
